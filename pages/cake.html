Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk

Estimated Time: 15 minutes

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix sugar and butter together while adding the eggs one at a time. Mix in vanilla.
  3. Combine flour and baking powder in a separate bowl. Add to the wet ingredients and mix well. Add milk and stir until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into the prepared cake pan.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until the top springs back when lightly touched. (30-40 minutes)
  6. Remove from the oven and allow to completely cool.
  7. Enjoy!
